The Doom of Chelchis Scout Rifle

The Doom of Chelchis is a Void-element Scout Rifle that drops from the King's Fall raid in Destiny 2. It is a strong Scout Rifle featuring a perk pool that can yield combinations such as Explosive Payload / Dragonfly, able to deal high amounts of damage while also creating an elemental explosion upon precision kills. Another interesting perk combination includes Firefly / Dragonfly for those Solar subclass Guardians who like to double-dip in precision kill explosions.

The Doom of Chelchis's full perk pool can be accessed on Light.gg.

Other King's Fall weapons include:
  • Smite of Merain (Pulse Rifle)
  • Defiance of Yasmin (Sniper Rifle)
  • Midha's Reckoning (Fusion Rifle)
  • Zaouli's Bane (Hand Cannon)
  • Qullim's Terminus (Machine Gun)
  • Touch of Malice (Exotic Scout Rifle)
